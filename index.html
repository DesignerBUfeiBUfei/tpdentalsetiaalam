DR KELVIN QUAH (I.D.G, USU)

A highly skilled dental surgeon with a decade of experience. Dr Kelvin graduated from Universitas Sumatera Utara in 2016. After returning to Malaysia, Dr Kelvin was posted to Langkawi. He served in Klinik Pergigian Air Hangat and Hospital Sultanah Maliha focusing on outpatient and trauma cases. Dr Kelvin also had the opportunity to serve as a junior Dental Officer at the Oral Maxillofacial Surgery Department and Paediatric Dental Department at Hospital Sultanah Bahiyah.

After leaving Ministry of Health in 2019, Dr Kelvin has been a full time private practioner. His focus is mainly on general dentistry, dentures and minor oral surgery.
